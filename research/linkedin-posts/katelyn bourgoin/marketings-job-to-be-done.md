# Marketing’s Job To Be Done

Source:  
https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/marketings-job-done-katelyn-bourgoin-barry-o-reilly

---

## Core Idea

Marketing should not begin with channels, campaigns, or content calendars.

It should begin with understanding why customers decide to change.

Katelyn Bourgoin explains that marketers need to study the forces that push buyers to seek new solutions, evaluate options, and finally make a decision.

---

## Key Insight

People do not buy products randomly.

They buy when something creates enough friction in their current situation.

Examples:

- current software is too expensive  
- workflows are too slow  
- tools are hard to use  
- team needs have changed  
- growth created new problems  

Marketing works better when it speaks to those moments.

---

## Why Reddit Matters for B2B SaaS

Although Reddit is not directly mentioned, Reddit is one of the best public places to observe these buying triggers in real time.

Users openly post things like:

- “Need an alternative to X tool”  
- “Our CRM pricing is insane now”  
- “Best project management software for a small team?”  
- “Anyone switched from X to Y?”  
- “This platform wastes too much time”

These are live buying signals.

---

## Buyer Research Framework

Katelyn’s framework can be applied to Reddit by analyzing threads for:

- frustrations with current tools  
- switching reasons  
- comparison requests  
- objections to pricing  
- desired outcomes  
- hidden use cases  

This turns Reddit into a customer research engine.

---

## Messaging Advantage

By studying Reddit conversations, SaaS marketers can improve:

- ad copy  
- landing page messaging  
- onboarding promises  
- feature positioning  
- competitor comparison pages  
- email campaigns  

Because the language comes from real users.

---

## B2B SaaS Application

A SaaS company can use subreddit discussions to discover:

- what pushes customers to leave competitors  
- what buyers fear before purchasing  
- what results users truly want  
- what product gaps exist in the market  

Then build campaigns around those truths.

---

## Extracted Pattern

Customer frustration → Public discussion → Market insight → Better messaging → Higher conversion

---

## Final Principle

Marketing’s job is not to interrupt people.

It is to understand why they are ready to change.
