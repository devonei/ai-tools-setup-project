# Ross Simmonds - LinkedIn Research Notes

## Summary
Ross Simmonds frequently shares insights about content distribution, audience growth, and maximizing reach from one content asset.

## Key Ideas Relevant to Reddit Marketing

### 1. Distribution Matters
Great content fails without smart distribution. This applies to Reddit where relevance and timing matter.

### 2. Add Value First
Communities respond better to useful contributions than promotion.

### 3. Repurpose Winning Topics
Questions and discussions from communities can inspire blogs, SEO pages, and social posts.

### 4. Build Trust Before Asking
Reddit users reject obvious self-promotion.

## Why This Matters for B2B SaaS
A SaaS company can use Reddit to understand customer pain points, answer questions, and build awareness before direct selling.
