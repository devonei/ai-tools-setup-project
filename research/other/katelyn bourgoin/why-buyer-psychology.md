# Why Buyer Psychology Is the Real Growth Hack

Source:  
https://www.forwardobsessed.com/episodes/katelyn-bourgoin

---

## Core Idea

Growth does not happen because people suddenly become interested.

It happens when buyers feel enough pain, friction, or urgency to finally make a change.

Katelyn Bourgoin explains that real growth comes from understanding buyer psychology rather than relying only on tactics or channels.

---

## Key Insight

People rarely switch products because of features alone.

They switch because something in their current situation becomes too frustrating.

Examples:

- current software is too expensive  
- workflow is too slow  
- support is terrible  
- team has outgrown old tools  
- manual processes waste time  

Pain creates momentum.

---

## Why Reddit Matters for B2B SaaS

Although Reddit is not directly discussed, Reddit is full of public buyer frustration.

Users constantly post:

- “Need an alternative to X tool”  
- “Why is this software so overpriced?”  
- “Anyone switched from X recently?”  
- “Best platform for a small startup?”  
- “This tool wastes too much time”

These conversations reveal real motivation to buy.

---

## Messaging Advantage

Katelyn’s framework suggests marketers should speak to pain first.

Reddit helps uncover the exact language customers use to describe that pain.

This improves:

- landing page copy  
- ad messaging  
- comparison pages  
- email campaigns  
- product positioning  

---

## Trust Principle

The episode also highlights authenticity and transparency.

That matters on Reddit because users quickly reject polished corporate promotion.

Helpful, honest participation performs better than forced marketing.

---

## B2B SaaS Application

A SaaS company can use Reddit discussions to discover:

- why users leave competitors  
- what problems matter most  
- what language resonates emotionally  
- what buying triggers appear repeatedly  
- what objections block conversions  

---

## Extracted Pattern

Pain appears publicly → Smart brands listen → Better messaging follows → Trust grows → Conversions improve

---

## Final Principle

Channels do not create demand alone.

Pain does.

The smartest marketers know where buyers talk about it.
