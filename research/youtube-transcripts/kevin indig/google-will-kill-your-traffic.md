# Google Will Kill Your Traffic - Here's How You Adapt | Kevin Indig (Growth Memo)

> Research notes from YouTube transcript — AI x SEO evolution, Google AI Overviews, and the future of search.

---

## 📌 Overview

This conversation between **Kevin Indig** and Ahrefs explores how AI is reshaping SEO from both sides:

- Search engines themselves (Google AI Overviews, AI Mode, SERP changes)
- Marketing workflows (AI used for SEO, content, and growth)

Core tension:
> Traffic is going down — but revenue is not necessarily collapsing.

---

## 🧠 Key Thesis

SEO is not dying.  
It is **changing shape under AI pressure**.

What used to be:
- 10 blue links
- keyword-driven content
- traffic as primary KPI

Is becoming:
- AI answers replacing clicks
- prompts replacing queries
- multi-platform discovery (Google + ChatGPT + Reddit + YouTube)

---

## 📉 Google Traffic Shift ("Crocodile Chart")

### Observations:
- Impressions ↑
- Clicks ↓
- Traffic overall: likely peaked around **2024**

Kevin’s take:
> 2024 may be remembered as peak web traffic era.

### Why impressions are rising:
- AI Overviews count citations as impressions
- Even "load more" expansion triggers impression tracking

---

## 🧪 AI Overview Behavior

- >85% of users click “load more” (study mentioned)
- Most users read **top third only**
- Behavior mirrors classic SERP scanning habits

Important implication:
> Being cited early in AI Overview = highest value placement

---

## 💰 Traffic vs Revenue Disconnect

Across industries:

| Industry | Impact |
|----------|--------|
| Publishers | ↓ revenue & traffic |
| Affiliates | mixed (decline + new spam winners) |
| SaaS | stable or ↑ revenue |
| E-commerce | minor AI impact (for now) |
| Marketplaces | slight decline |

### Key insight:
- Traffic loss ≠ revenue loss
- Attribution is breaking (people just say “Google”)

---

## 🤖 AI Overviews Coverage

- Mostly informational queries (~99% historically)
- Emerging presence in:
  - comparison queries
  - “best X for Y” queries
  - some transactional intent

Conclusion:
> AI Overviews are expanding beyond top-of-funnel faster than most tools can measure.

---

## 🧩 How to Rank in AI Overviews

Core ranking signals (still SEO fundamentals):

### 1. Relevance
- Match intent precisely
- Long-tail specificity wins

### 2. Freshness
- Updated, recent, maintained content

### 3. Authority / Trust
- Brand mentions across the web matter more than ever

### 4. External validation
- Reddit, YouTube, forums, review sites increasingly influence AI answers

---

## 🌐 The New SEO Reality: Multi-Platform Trust

AI systems pull from:
- Reddit (Google-heavy bias)
- Wikipedia (ChatGPT/Perplexity bias)
- YouTube
- Review platforms (G2, Trustpilot, etc.)

Implication:
> Your brand is no longer “ranked on Google”
> It is “voted on across the internet”

---

## 🧠 SEO Is Not Dead — It’s Repricing

SEO is shifting from:

- “rank pages → get clicks”

to:

- “be referenced → influence decisions”

Leadership perception:
- Traffic ↓ = SEO crisis signal
- But revenue often tells a different story

---

## ⚙️ AI for SEO (Workflow Revolution)

### 1. Sales call mining → content creation

Example (Ramp case):
- Analyze customer calls with AI
- Extract:
  - pain points
  - objections
  - buying triggers
- Turn into content with higher conversion rate

Insight:
> Not keyword-led content — but conversation-led content

---

### 2. AI-generated interactive tools

- calculators
- quizzes
- widgets
- micro-apps inside content

Impact:
- higher engagement
- higher conversion
- better differentiation in SERPs + AI citations

---

### 3. Workflow automation (not full replacement)

AI is best at:
- steps inside workflows
- not entire workflows

Example:
- keyword research → partially AI assisted
- content drafts → AI assisted
- editing + strategy → human still required

---

## 🧨 “SEO Is Dead” Narrative

Kevin’s stance:

- SEO has “died” multiple times before
- This time is NOT death — it’s compression + redistribution

Reality:
- SEO demand inside companies is increasing
- Budgets are shifting toward AI visibility

---

## 📊 AI Search vs ChatGPT Visibility

Differences:

### Google AI Overviews:
- SERP-driven
- intent mapped via keywords
- heavily dynamic

### ChatGPT:
- Bing + web grounding influence
- more brand/entity driven
- fragmented market = more volatility

Key insight:
> Same brand can dominate Google but not ChatGPT (and vice versa)

---

## 🧬 Strategic Shift for Brands

To survive AI search:

### You must optimize:
- what your website says
- what OTHER websites say about you
- how your brand is described across platforms

This includes:
- Reddit discussions
- Wikipedia presence
- review platforms
- forums & communities

---

## 🔥 Final Takeaways

- SEO is not dying — it is evolving into **AI-mediated discovery**
- Traffic is becoming a weaker success metric
- Brand presence across the web matters more than ever
- Content must be tied to **action**, not just information
- AI accelerates everything — good SEO becomes more powerful, not less

---

## 🧾 Closing Thought

> “It’s never as bad as it seems, and never as good as it seems.”

SEO future = middle ground:
- not collapse
- not utopia
- but transformation
