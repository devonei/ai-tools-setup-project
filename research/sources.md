# Research Sources
Topic: Reddit Marketing for B2B SaaS

## Overview
This project focuses on identifying high-signal experts whose work can help build a practical Reddit marketing playbook for B2B SaaS companies.

I selected experts based on:
- Proven growth marketing experience
- Strong public educational content
- Practical advice over generic theory
- Relevance to community-led growth, audience research, and organic acquisition
