# Reddit Marketing for B2B SaaS — Research Project

## Overview

This repository is a structured research system analyzing how Reddit is used as a modern distribution, discovery, and trust-building channel for B2B SaaS companies.

The goal is not to collect content, but to extract patterns from real practitioners and understand how Reddit influences buyer behavior, SEO visibility, and AI-driven discovery systems.

---

## Why Reddit Marketing?

Reddit has evolved beyond a discussion forum into a critical layer of the modern internet ecosystem:

- It frequently appears in Google search results for high-intent queries
- It influences AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini
- It contains peer-driven discussions that strongly affect B2B purchase decisions

For SaaS companies, Reddit functions as a **trust and intent validation layer**, not just a social platform.

---

## Why These Experts Were Selected

The selected experts were chosen based on **real-world practitioner relevance**, not popularity.

Criteria used:
- Active experience in SaaS growth or distribution
- Demonstrated understanding of Reddit or community-led marketing
- Evidence of applied strategies (not purely theoretical content)
- Multi-channel expertise (LinkedIn, YouTube, podcasts, frameworks)

---

## Experts Included

### Ross Simmonds
Selected for his deep expertise in content distribution systems and practical Reddit marketing frameworks. His work consistently connects SEO, Reddit visibility, and modern B2B buyer behavior.

---

## What Was Collected

For each expert, multiple content types were gathered and structured:

- LinkedIn posts → distribution insights and marketing philosophy
- YouTube videos → spoken frameworks and strategic breakdowns
- Podcast interviews → applied Reddit marketing strategies
- Framework pages → structured systems (e.g., Reddit Blueprint)

---

## Repository Structure

- `/research/sources.md` → Master index of all sources and experts
- `/research/linkedin-posts/` → Structured analysis of LinkedIn content
- `/research/youtube-transcripts/` → Video and lecture breakdowns
- `/research/other/` → Frameworks, podcasts, and external resources

---

## Key Research Focus

This project focuses on identifying patterns in:

- Reddit as a SaaS distribution channel
- Trust-building through community participation
- Content seeding vs direct promotion strategies
- Influence of Reddit on SEO and AI systems
- Buyer journey shifts toward peer-driven platforms

---

## Next Step

This dataset will be used to synthesize a Reddit marketing playbook for B2B SaaS, based on recurring patterns across practitioners rather than isolated opinions.
